Gyoza Dumplings

Dumplings and dipping sauce

Ingredients

  • 50 dumpling wrappers
  • 1 Tbsp vegetable oil for each batch
  • 1 tsp roasted sesame oil
  • ¼ cup water

Filling

  • ¾ lb ground pork
  • 5 oz green cabbage
  • 2 green onion/scallion
  • 2 shiitake mushrooms
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 knob ginger (1 inch)
  • 1 tsp sake
  • 1 tsp roasted sesame oil
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • ¼ tsp kosher salt
  • ⅛ tsp freshly ground black pepper

Dipping Sauce

  • 1 Tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • Optional: 1/2 tsp Szechuan oil
