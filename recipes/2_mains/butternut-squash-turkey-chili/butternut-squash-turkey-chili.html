Butternut Squash and Turkey Chili

Category: Meal Prep

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound ground turkey breast
  • 1 pound butternut squash - peeled, seeded and cut into 1-inch dice
  • Â½ cup chicken broth
  • 1 (4.5 ounce) can chopped green chilies
  • 2 (14.5 ounce) cans petite diced tomatoes
  • 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans with liquid
  • 1 (15.5 ounce) can white hominy, drained
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin (try all splice)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Stir in the onion and garlic; cook and stir for 3 minutes.
  2. Add turkey, and stir until crumbly and no longer pink.
  3. Add remaining ingredients and bring to a simmer. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the squash is tender, about 20 minutes.
