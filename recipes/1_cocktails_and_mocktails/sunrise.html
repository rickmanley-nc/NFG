Sunrise

Tequila, Bourbon, Rum, Cognac, Vodka

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces tequila/bourbon/rum/cognac/vodka
  • 4 ounces orange/pineapplek juice
  • 1/4 ounce grenadine
  • Garnish: sword + orange slice and cherry

Steps

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice.
  2. Add alcohol and juice.
  3. Top with the grenadine, which sinks to the bottom of the glass, creating a layered effect.
  4. Garnish with an orange/pineapple slice and a cherry.
