instructions:
  - group: Preparation
    steps:
      - step: "[PLACEHOLDER] Remove membrane from back of ribs using a paper towel for grip"
      - step: "[PLACEHOLDER] Apply thin layer of yellow mustard to all sides"
      - step: "[PLACEHOLDER] Mix all rub ingredients and coat ribs generously"
      - step: "[PLACEHOLDER] Let ribs sit at room temperature for 30 minutes"
  - group: 3-2-1 Method
    steps:
      - step: "[PLACEHOLDER] Preheat smoker to 225°F with fruit wood (apple or cherry)"
      - step: "[PLACEHOLDER] Smoke ribs bone side down for 3 hours, spritzing every 45 minutes after first hour"
      - step: "[PLACEHOLDER] Wrap ribs in foil with a splash of apple juice - smoke for 2 hours"
      - step: "[PLACEHOLDER] Unwrap and return to smoker for 1 hour to firm up bark"
      - step: "[PLACEHOLDER] Brush with BBQ sauce in last 15-20 minutes if desired"
  - group: Finishing
    steps:
      - step: "[PLACEHOLDER] Ribs are done when meat has pulled back from bones about 1/4 inch"
      - step: "[PLACEHOLDER] Let rest 10 minutes before cutting"
      - step: "[PLACEHOLDER] Cut between bones and serve"
notes: "[PLACEHOLDER] Ricky will add personal notes here. The 3-2-1 method is a guideline - baby backs may need less time (try 2-2-1). Ribs should bend but not break when lifted with tongs."
equipment:
  - Smoker or Big Green Egg
  - Spray bottle
  - Heavy duty aluminum foil
  - Thermapen or meat thermometer
