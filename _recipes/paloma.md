---
layout: recipe
title: Paloma
subtitle: Tequila, Grapefruit, Lime
category: cocktails
prep_time: 5 minutes
servings: 1
images:
  - path: assets/img/portfolio/paloma.jpg
    alt: Paloma Cocktail
ingredients:
  - group: Cocktail
    items:
      - item: tequila blanco
        amount: 2
        unit: ounces
      - item: lime juice
        amount: 1/2
        unit: ounce
        note: freshly squeezed
      - item: grapefruit soda
        amount: 3
        unit: ounces
        note: or fresh grapefruit juice plus soda water
      - item: salt
        note: for rim (optional)
      - item: lime wedge
        note: for garnish
instructions:
  - group: Cocktail
    steps:
      - step: If desired, rim a highball glass with salt
      - step: Fill the glass with ice and add tequila and lime juice
      - step: Top with grapefruit soda and stir gently to combine
      - step: Garnish with a lime wedge and serve
notes: If using fresh grapefruit juice instead of soda, add a splash of soda water and a small amount of sweetener to balance bitterness.
equipment:
  - Highball glass
  - Jigger
  - Bar spoon
---
