---
layout: recipe
title: Old Fashioned
subtitle: Bourbon or Rye, Angostura, Orange
category: cocktails
prep_time: 5 minutes
servings: 1
images:
  - path: assets/img/portfolio/old-fashioned.jpg
    alt: Old Fashioned Cocktail
ingredients:
  - group: Cocktail
    items:
      - item: bourbon or rye whiskey
        amount: 2
        unit: ounces
      - item: sugar cube
        amount: 1
        unit: cube
      - item: Angostura bitters
        amount: 2
        unit: dashes
      - item: water
        amount: 1
        unit: teaspoon
        note: optional, to help dissolve sugar
      - item: orange peel
        note: for garnish
      - item: cherry
        note: for garnish
instructions:
  - group: Cocktail
    steps:
      - step: Place the sugar cube in a rocks glass
      - step: Add bitters and water, then muddle until the sugar is mostly dissolved
      - step: Add whiskey and a large ice cube, then stir until chilled
      - step: Express the orange peel over the glass and drop it in
      - step: Garnish with a cherry and serve
notes: For a classic profile, use rye whiskey for a drier finish or bourbon for a sweeter finish. Adjust bitters to taste.
equipment:
  - Rocks glass
  - Muddler
  - Bar spoon
---
