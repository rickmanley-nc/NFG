---
layout: recipe
title: Swiss Chocolate Cake
subtitle: A rich, moist chocolate cake with a gooey pecan and chocolate topping
category: desserts
prep_time: 15 minutes
cook_time: 30 minutes
cooling_time: 20 minutes
servings: 12 slices
images:
  - path: assets/img/portfolio/swiss-chocolate-cake.jpg
    alt: Swiss Chocolate Cake
ingredients:
  - group: Cake
    items:
      - item: German chocolate cake mix
        amount: 1
        unit: box
      - item: small box vanilla instant pudding mix
        amount: 1
        unit: box
        note: 3.4 oz
      - item: eggs
        amount: 4
        unit: large
      - item: sour cream
        amount: 1
        unit: cup
      - item: vegetable oil
        amount: 1/2
        unit: cup
      - item: milk
        amount: 1 1/2
        unit: cups
  - group: Topping
    items:
      - item: chopped pecans
        amount: 1
        unit: cup
      - item: Hershey chocolate bars
        amount: 2
        unit: bars
        note: roughly chopped
instructions:
  - group: Preparation
    steps:
      - step: Preheat oven to 325°F. Grease and flour a 9x13-inch baking pan.
      - step: Roughly chop the Hershey chocolate bars and set aside with the chopped pecans for the topping.
  - group: Make the Batter
    steps:
      - step: In a large mixing bowl, cream together the cake mix, vanilla instant pudding mix, eggs, sour cream, vegetable oil, and milk until well combined and smooth.
      - step: Fold the batter gently until it is uniform, being careful not to overmix.
      - step: Pour the batter evenly into the prepared baking pan.
  - group: Bake and Finish
    steps:
      - step: Bake at 325°F for approximately 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Bake time may vary depending on your oven.
      - step: Remove cake from the oven and allow it to cool for about 20 minutes.
      - step: Once cooled, scatter the chopped pecans and chopped Hershey chocolate bar pieces evenly over the top of the cake.
      - step: Allow the chocolate pieces to soften slightly from the residual heat, then serve.
notes: This recipe uses a box cake mix for an easy, consistently moist result. The vanilla pudding adds extra richness. The Hershey chocolate bar topping will soften and partially melt on the warm cake — do not add topping while cake is piping hot or it will melt completely.
equipment:
  - 9x13-inch baking pan
  - Large mixing bowl
  - Electric mixer or stand mixer
  - Measuring cups and spoons
  - Toothpick (for testing doneness)
---
