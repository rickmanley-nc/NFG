---
layout: recipe
title: Instant Pot - Cheesecake
subtitle: Sounds crazy, but trust us, it's delicious!
category: desserts
prep_time: 20 minutes
cook_time: 35 minutes
cooling_time: 4-8 hours
servings: 8 slices
images:
  - path: assets/img/portfolio/instant-pot-cheesecake.jpg
    alt: Instant Pot Cheesecake
ingredients:
  - group: Crust
    items:
      - item: graham cracker crumbs
        amount: 1
        unit: cup
      - item: unsalted butter
        amount: 3
        unit: tablespoons
        note: melted
      - item: granulated sugar
        amount: 1
        unit: tablespoon
  - group: Filling
    items:
      - item: cream cheese
        amount: 16
        unit: oz
        note: two 8-oz blocks, room temperature
      - item: granulated sugar
        amount: 1/2
        unit: cup
      - item: sour cream
        amount: 1/4
        unit: cup
        note: room temperature
      - item: vanilla extract
        amount: 1
        unit: teaspoon
      - item: eggs
        amount: 2
        unit: large
        note: room temperature
instructions:
  - group: Prepare the Crust
    steps:
      - step: Mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar in a bowl until combined.
      - step: Press the mixture into the bottom of a 7-inch springform pan, creating an even layer.
      - step: Place the pan in the freezer while you prepare the filling.
  - group: Make the Filling
    steps:
      - step: Beat cream cheese with an electric mixer until smooth and fluffy, about 2-3 minutes.
      - step: Add sugar and beat until combined, scraping down the sides as needed.
      - step: Mix in sour cream and vanilla extract until smooth.
      - step: Add eggs one at a time, beating on low speed just until combined. Do not overmix.
  - group: Pressure Cook
    steps:
      - step: Pour the filling over the crust in the springform pan.
      - step: Cover the pan tightly with aluminum foil.
      - step: Add 1.5 cups of water to the Instant Pot and place the trivet inside.
      - step: Create a foil sling to lower the pan onto the trivet and help remove it later.
      - step: Close the lid, set the valve to sealing, and cook on High Pressure for 35 minutes.
      - step: When done, let pressure release naturally for 10 minutes, then quick release remaining pressure.
  - group: Cool and Serve
    steps:
      - step: Carefully remove the cheesecake using the foil sling.
      - step: Remove the foil cover and blot any water from the surface with a paper towel.
      - step: Let cool at room temperature for 1 hour, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
      - step: Run a knife around the edges before releasing the springform ring.
notes: |
  Tips for the perfect Instant Pot cheesecake:
  - All dairy ingredients should be at room temperature for a smooth filling
  - Don't overmix once eggs are added — this prevents cracking
  - The foil cover prevents water from dripping onto the cheesecake
  - For extra richness, top with fresh berries, chocolate sauce, or caramel
equipment:
  - Instant Pot (6-quart or larger)
  - 7-inch springform pan
  - Trivet (usually included with Instant Pot)
  - Electric mixer
  - Aluminum foil
variations:
  - Add 2 tablespoons cocoa powder for chocolate cheesecake
  - Swap graham crackers for Oreo cookie crumbs for a cookies & cream version
  - Add lemon zest and juice for lemon cheesecake
  - Top with fresh strawberries or a berry compote before serving
