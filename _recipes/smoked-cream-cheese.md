title: Smoked Cream Cheese
subtitle: The Viral Appetizer Everyone Loves
category: smoked
prep_time: 10 minutes
cook_time: 2 hours
servings: 8-10
images:
  - path: assets/img/portfolio/smoked-cream-cheese.jpg
    alt: Smoked Cream Cheese Appetizer
ingredients:
  - group: Main
    items:
      - item: cream cheese
        amount: 2
        unit: blocks
        note: 8 oz each, cold
      - item: BBQ rub
        amount: 2
        unit: tablespoons
        note: your favorite, sweet or savory
      - item: olive oil
        amount: 1
        unit: tablespoon
        note: for coating
  - group: Optional Toppings
    items:
      - item: everything bagel seasoning
        note: alternative to BBQ rub
      - item: jalapeño jelly
        note: for serving
      - item: hot honey
        note: for drizzling
      - item: bacon bits
        note: for garnish
      - item: green onions
        note: chopped, for garnish
instructions:
  - group: Preparation
    steps:
      - step: "[PLACEHOLDER] Score the top of each cream cheese block in a crosshatch pattern (don't cut all the way through)"
      - step: "[PLACEHOLDER] Brush lightly with olive oil"
      - step: "[PLACEHOLDER] Apply BBQ rub generously to all sides, getting into the scored lines"
  - group: Smoking
    steps:
      - step: "[PLACEHOLDER] Preheat smoker to 225°F"
      - step: "[PLACEHOLDER] Place cream cheese directly on the grate or on a small piece of foil with edges"
      - step: "[PLACEHOLDER] Smoke for 2 hours until the cream cheese is soft, slightly melted at edges, and has golden color"
      - step: "[PLACEHOLDER] The rub should form a nice crust on top"
  - group: Serving
    steps:
      - step: "[PLACEHOLDER] Transfer to a serving plate"
      - step: "[PLACEHOLDER] Drizzle with jalapeño jelly or hot honey if desired"
      - step: "[PLACEHOLDER] Serve immediately with crackers, pretzels, or sliced baguette"
notes: "[PLACEHOLDER] Ricky will add personal notes here. This is a great low-effort appetizer that always impresses. The cream cheese should be cold when it goes on the smoker so it holds its shape."
equipment:
  - Smoker or Big Green Egg
