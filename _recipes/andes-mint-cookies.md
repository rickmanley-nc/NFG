---
layout: recipe
title: Andes Mint Cookies
subtitle: Rich chocolate cookies topped with melted Andes mints
category: desserts
prep_time: 20 minutes
cook_time: 10 minutes
cooling_time: 10 minutes
servings: 36 cookies
images:
  - path: assets/img/portfolio/andes-mint-cookies.jpg
    alt: Andes Mint Cookies
ingredients:
  - group: Cookies
    items:
      - item: all-purpose flour
        amount: 1 1/2
        unit: cups
      - item: unsweetened cocoa powder
        amount: 1/2
        unit: cup
      - item: baking soda
        amount: 1
        unit: teaspoon
      - item: salt
        amount: 1/4
        unit: teaspoon
      - item: unsalted butter
        amount: 3/4
        unit: cup
        note: softened
      - item: brown sugar
        amount: 1
        unit: cup
        note: packed
      - item: granulated sugar
        amount: 1/2
        unit: cup
      - item: eggs
        amount: 2
        unit: large
      - item: vanilla extract
        amount: 1
        unit: teaspoon
      - item: semisweet chocolate chips
        amount: 1
        unit: cup
  - group: Topping
    items:
      - item: Andes mints
        amount: 36
        unit: pieces
        note: unwrapped
instructions:
  - group: Preparation
    steps:
      - step: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
      - step: In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  - group: Mix Cookie Dough
    steps:
      - step: In a large bowl, cream together butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
      - step: Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla extract.
      - step: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until just combined.
      - step: Fold in semisweet chocolate chips.
  - group: Bake and Finish
    steps:
      - step: Scoop rounded tablespoons of dough onto prepared baking sheets, spacing about 2 inches apart.
      - step: Bake for 8-10 minutes, until the cookies are set around the edges.
      - step: Remove cookies from the oven and immediately press one Andes mint into the center of each cookie.
      - step: Let the mints soften for 1-2 minutes, then gently spread the melted chocolate over the tops.
      - step: Allow cookies to cool completely so the topping can set.
notes: Store in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days.
equipment:
  - Mixing bowls
  - Electric mixer
  - Measuring cups and spoons
  - Baking sheets
  - Parchment paper
---
